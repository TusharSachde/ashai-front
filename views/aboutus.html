Ishaan Kapoor, CEO

Ishaan focuses on finding the best Non-Profits & Projects that need support

There’s millions of problems out there that need solving, problems that are incredibly apparent and we see every day. The great part is that if everyone of us puts in just a little bit, we can make a real impact and change the way the world works. To me, Power for One is about creating that change. It’s about getting people involved, it’s about making companies responsible and it’s about helping NGOs become efficient. In my spare time, you’ll find me watching a Liverpool game or reading historical fiction.
